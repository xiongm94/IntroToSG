A diner founded and opened in the 1950s by Mike McCalister. Get your day started with a stuffed omelete with some hash and some OJ or grab yourself a fresh brew of coffee. If you are hungry, we also serve a variety of burgers with steak fries or fish n' chips. Don't forget to grab a good old fashioned malt shake for those sweet tooth cravings.
